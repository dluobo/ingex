The preferred way to contact the developers of Ingex is via the SourceForge forums. If you believe you have found a bug in Ingex please submit a bug report. Patches and other contributions are welcome.

The Ingex project is actively developed as part of the Production Automation work managed by David Kirby of BBC Research.

The Ingex software was written by Stuart Cunningham, Philip de Nier, John Fletcher, Matthew Marks, Rowan de Pomerai and Jim Easterbrook. Feedback is welcome.
